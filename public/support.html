Is DOGSWAB medical advice?

No. DOGSWAB provides educational information only. Always consult your veterinarian for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí DOGSWAB ‚Üí Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund for your subscription.

How do I add multiple pets?

In the app, go to Pet Management ‚Üí Add Pet. You can add unlimited pets on all subscription plans.

Is my pet's data secure?

Yes. We use HIPAA-level security with end-to-end encryption to protect your pet's health information.

Contact your veterinarian or emergency animal hospital immediately. DOGSWAB cannot replace professional emergency care.
